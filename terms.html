6 Mobile & DTH Recharge Services

When you initiate a recharge, the App routes your request through an authorized recharge API provider and the respective operator. Recharges are typically processed instantly but may take up to 24 hours in exceptional cases.

Always double-check the mobile number, operator, and plan amount before confirming. Successful recharges cannot be reversed.
